About Us

Upvan Dhaba, established in 2020, by Shubham Singh offers an exceptional dining experience that blends traditional Indian, Chinese, Tandoori, and Continental cuisines. Our goal is to provide authentic flavors with a touch of innovation, ensuring that each meal is prepared to perfection. The warm, welcoming ambiance of our restaurant is designed to make you feel at home, whether you're enjoying a meal with family or celebrating a special occasion. At Upvan Dhaba, we prioritize not just great food but also an unforgettable experience that keeps you coming back for more.
